Our Story

Hosting: Built by (ex) Developers for Developers

Corefinity was founded by Magento developers who were frustrated with slow, inflexible hosting and set out to build the fastest, most secure ecommerce platform.

Our platform is engineered by certified developers and engineers with years of hands-on experience supporting Magento, Shopware and many other platforms.

Every part of the stack is developer and agency friendly, with the tools, documentation and support you need to ship quickly and scale confidently. This is hosting created by people who use to build stores every day, trusted by hundreds of merchants to power their success.
