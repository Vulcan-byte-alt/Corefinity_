Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Agency Partner Programme.

We welcome agencies of all sizes. The main requirements are: an active agency business serving UK clients, a commitment to quality web development, and a desire to provide excellent hosting services to your clients. We have three partnership tiers (Silver, Gold, Platinum) based on the number of clients you bring to the platform.

Partners earn recurring monthly commission on all active client hosting accounts. Commission rates range from 15% (Silver) to 25% (Platinum) depending on your partnership tier. Commissions are paid automatically on the 1st of each month for the previous month's hosting revenue.

Our UK-based developer support team provides assistance with server configuration, deployment issues, performance optimization, security concerns, and technical troubleshooting. Support is available via phone, email, and live chat. Average response time is under 15 minutes for critical issues.

Each partnership tier includes monthly development hours (3-10 hours depending on tier) that can be used for site maintenance, updates, security patches, or improvements. Hours are banked and roll over month-to-month, so you can accumulate hours for larger projects. This budget helps you provide ongoing value to clients without eating into your margins.

This includes a full audit of the current setup, migration planning, data transfer, testing, and DNS cutover. For subsequent migrations, Silver partners receive 1 free migration per year, Gold partners get 3, and Platinum partners receive unlimited free migrations.

Absolutely. Gold and Platinum partners have access to white-label options including custom nameservers, branded control panel, and the ability to present the hosting service under your own agency brand. We work behind the scenes to ensure your clients see your agency as their hosting provider.

There are no long-term contracts or commitments. If you decide to leave the partnership, you can do so with 30 days notice. Your clients can choose to continue with Corefinity directly, or we can assist with migration to another provider. Any earned commissions will be paid out as normal during the notice period.
